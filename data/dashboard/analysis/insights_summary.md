## Business Insights Summary

### Performance Overview
- Total sales exceed $2.3M, with an overall profit margin of approximately 12%.
- Sales volume is strong, but profitability is uneven across products and categories.

### Regional & Category Insights
- The West and East regions contribute the largest share of total sales and profit.
- Technology products generate the highest profit margins compared to Furniture and Office Supplies.

### Product-Level Insights
- A small number of products account for a significant portion of total profit.
- Several products consistently generate losses and negatively impact overall profitability.

### Discount Impact
- Higher discount levels are strongly associated with reduced or negative profit.
- Loss-making products are often heavily discounted, indicating pricing or cost structure issues.

### Business Recommendations
- Review pricing and discount strategies for underperforming products.
- Focus inventory and marketing efforts on high-margin products.
- Investigate cost reduction opportunities for products with persistent losses.
