# Sales Performance Analysis & Executive Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes retail sales data to evaluate overall business performance, profitability drivers, and loss-making areas. The objective is to transform raw transactional data into executive-ready insights using Excel and Power BI.

## Business Objectives
- Measure overall sales and profitability performance
- Track key operational and financial KPIs
- Analyze regional and category-level performance
- Identify high-performing and loss-making products
- Assess the impact of discounting on profitability

## Tools & Technologies
- Excel (data cleaning and validation)
- Power BI (DAX, data modeling, dashboard design)

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Orders (Distinct)
- Average Order Value

## Dashboard Pages
1. Executive Overview  
   - High-level KPIs and monthly sales trend

2. Regional & Category Performance  
   - Sales and profit comparison by region and product category

3. Product & Discount Insights  
   - Top and bottom products by profit  
   - Profit versus discount analysis

## Key Insights
- Profit is highly concentrated among a small number of products.
- Several products consistently generate losses and require pricing or cost review.
- Higher discount levels are strongly associated with reduced or negative profitability.

## Repository Structure
- `data/` – Cleaned dataset used for analysis  
- `dashboard/` – Power BI dashboard file  
- `analysis/` – Business insights and written summaries
